United States - States & Counties

Complete and extend Administrative hierarchy

(missing from base install)

The module adds all official United States political and geographic regions - states, territories, counties/city-counties, parishes (Louisiana) and boroughs (Alaska).

When entering an address, once a valid state is chosen, the county equivalent is filtered.

A new menu at Sales --> Configuration --> Address Book --> Localization --> USA Counties is added to maintain this data.
